TRIANGLE TRACKER

Definition

A triangle is a three sided polygon.It is one of the basic shapes in geometry.

Types of a triangle

There are three types of a triangle mainly;
  • Equilateral Triangle
  • Isosceles
  • Scalene

Equilateral Triangle

An Equilateral Triangle is a type of triangle that all sides are equal.

Isosceles Triangle

An Isosceles Triangle is a type of triangle that only two sides are equal.

Scalene

A Scalene is a triangle that neither of the sides are equal.
